Spaghetti

Description

Everyone has their preferred version of Spaghetti. Different thickness of noodle, different types/amounts of spices, etc. This is my favorite version.

Ingredients

  • 3 cans tomato sauce
  • 1 lb angel hair pasta
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • pinch of sugar
  • oregano to taste
  • basil to taste
  • garlic powder to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Cook beef in pot on high
  3. Lower to medium and add sauce
  4. Season to taste
