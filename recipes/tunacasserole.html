Tuna Casserole

Description

This is the Tuna Casserole my mother would make for our family. It is a delicous dish that is easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans tuna
  • 1/2 cup Mayo
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 6 oz no yolk noodles
  • 2 cans cream of chicken soup
  • onion powder to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Add all other ingredients together in microwave safe dish
  3. Add noodles to mixture
  4. Microwave for 10 minutes
