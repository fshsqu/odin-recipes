Mac and Cheese

Description

This Mac and Cheese will make your tastebuds feel amazing all the way up until you're sick to your stomach.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb pasta
  • A LOT of Velveeta
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese
  • Pinch of salt
  • Your preferred hot sauce, to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil noodles
  2. Cut up Velveeta into cubes, add to hot pasta
  3. Add cheddar cheese
  4. Add remaining ingredients to taste
